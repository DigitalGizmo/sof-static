Colonial Northeast, circa 1660 - 1725

This map shows English and French settlements and a few Native village sites that were well-known to Europeans between 1660 and 1725. Present-day political boundaries (dotted lines) are included to help orient modern readers.

Sources: Captors and Captives: The 1704 French and Indian Raid on Deerfield, by Evan Haefeli and Kevin Sweeney, 2003; and information from advisors to this website.
