"A Map of New England" by John Foster, 1677
John Foster's map, published in a history of King Philip's War, shows settlements affected by the conflict.

Use the "+" and "-" buttons in the bottom left corner of the map to zoom in and out. Once zoomed in, click and hold down your mouse button near the edge of the image to pan left and right, up and down.

Courtesy of the Osher Map Library and Smith Center for Cartographic Education at the University of Southern Maine
