Kanienkehaka Homelands
The homeland of the Kanienkehaka is Kanienkeh, "land of the flint"—from the Mohawk River on the south to the St. Lawrence on the north, and from Lake Champlain and the Richelieu River on the east to the Oneida nation on the west.


Source: 7 Generations, by David Blanchard, 1980 and information from advisors to this website.
